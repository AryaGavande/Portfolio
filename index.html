Selected Tableau Projects

Three examples of how I turn operational and business data into structured visual stories.

Business Intelligence

Automotive Service Analysis

Challenge: Service data covered revenue, repair time, vehicle models, billing types, mileage, and customer complaints, making relationships between performance factors difficult to interpret.

Approach: I analyzed the service records and created an interactive Tableau dashboard to compare the key variables. I also used statistical and machine-learning methods to identify the factors most strongly associated with service outcomes.

Outcome: The project translated operational data into a visual summary of the factors influencing revenue and service performance, making high-performing segments and operational concerns easier to identify.

Data Visualization

Customer Service Analysis

Challenge: Customer interactions occurred across multiple stages of the dealership journey, making it difficult to see where experiences were positive and where customers encountered friction.

Approach: I organized customer-service data into a visual journey and developed a Tableau story connecting customer behavior, service interactions, and experience indicators. The sequence guides the audience through the journey rather than presenting disconnected charts.

Outcome: The project created a clearer view of the customer experience and highlighted stages where service quality, communication, or process improvements could have the greatest effect.

View on Tableau

Data Storytelling

Profit Margin Analysis

Challenge: Overall revenue figures could hide meaningful differences in profitability across products, categories, regions, and business segments.

Approach: I developed a Tableau story to examine profit margins across key business dimensions and compare areas of strong performance with areas where revenue did not translate into proportionate profit.

Outcome: The analysis transformed profitability data into a focused visual narrative, showing which segments contributed most to margin performance and where further investigation or cost control may be needed.
